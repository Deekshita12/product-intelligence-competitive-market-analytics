# 📊 Dashboard Screenshots

This folder contains screenshots of the interactive Power BI dashboards developed for the **Product Intelligence & Competitive Market Analytics** project.

The dashboards provide a comprehensive view of marketplace performance, competitive positioning, pricing strategy and business opportunities through interactive visualizations and KPI-driven insights.

---

## Dashboard Pages

| Screenshot | Dashboard | Description |
|------------|-----------|-------------|
| **01_cover_page.png** | Cover Page | Project introduction, technology stack, and navigation. |
| **02_executive_overview.png** | Executive Overview | High-level marketplace KPIs, category performance, and executive summary. |
| **03_competitive_intelligence.png** | Competitive Intelligence & Market Positioning | Competitive benchmarking, market saturation, hidden opportunities, and category analysis. |
| **04_product_intelligence.png** | Product Intelligence & Pricing Analytics | Product pricing, discount analysis, customer ratings, value score, and pricing strategy. |
| **05_market_opportunity.png** | Market Opportunity & Strategic Insights | Strategic recommendations, hidden market opportunities, premium segments, and executive insights. |

---

## Dashboard Features

- 📈 Interactive KPI Cards
- 📊 Dynamic Charts & Visualizations
- 🎯 Category-Level Analysis
- 💰 Pricing & Discount Analysis
- 📌 Competitive Benchmarking
- 📉 Market Saturation Analysis
